TERRAIN CHALLENGES

Sometimes the map's just being a pain and nothing wants to line up right.

Later levels introduce terrain features that affect signal transmission.

  • hills that weaken signals

  • valleys that distort range

  • obstacles that block tower placement

  • environmental interference zones

So you'll have to get creative and figure out how to route around all this stuff that gets in your way.

LEVEL PROGRESSION

From simple connections to complex networks.

Early levels focus on understanding signal range and placement.

  • larger landscapes

  • stricter tower limits

  • complex terrain interference

  • multi-path routing challenges

After a while you stop just throwing towers around and actually start thinking about where to put them.
